Core Features

Local‑First

Your world lives on your device and syncs only when you choose to.

AI Memory

Recall conversations and files using built‑in embeddings and summarization.

Design Skins

Customize typography, colours and layout; share your theme with the world.

Portable Worlds

Export your entire world to a portable package and import it anywhere.

Modular Architecture

WorldOS is built from thin, powerful modules so you can extend and adapt it to your needs.

Presence

Real‑time chat, posts and reactions with CRDTs for conflict‑free collaboration.

Memory

Local embeddings power retrieval‑augmented prompts, summarization and search.

Theming

Create your own look and feel with shareable skins.

Sync & Identity

Optional peer‑to‑peer or relay sync with per‑world key pairs and role‑scoped tokens.

Security & Privacy

Signed Writes

Every change is signed with your world and device keys for tamper‑proof history.

Granular Permissions

Capability tokens grant fine‑grained access to members based on roles.

Privacy Modes

Choose sealed, invite or open modes to control how your world is shared.

Extensions & SDK

Build on WorldOS through a secure sandbox. Declare the capabilities you need — key‑value storage, file read/write, event subscriptions, AI queries and custom UI panels — and create rich plug‑ins.

A marketplace connects your creations with the community. Monetize premium extensions or offer them for free.

Get Started

Ready to build your own world? Join the builder program, contribute extensions or just explore our sample worlds. Contact us to learn more.
