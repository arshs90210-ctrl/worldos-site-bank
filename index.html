Security & Privacy

Extensions & SDK

Build on WorldOS through a secure sandbox. Declare the capabilities you need — key‑value storage, file read/write, event subscriptions, AI queries and custom UI panels — and create rich plug‑ins.

A marketplace connects your creations with the community. Monetize premium extensions or offer them for free.

WorldOS Data Bank

Transform your information into a protected, productive asset. Deposit data into your personal vault, record a Proof of Deposit ledger, and choose when to share or earn insights from your knowledge.

WorldOS is your personal data bank — deposit, verify, and own your information like currency.

Vault

Local, encrypted storage for your chats, documents and media. Each deposit generates a unique Proof‑of‑Deposit (PoD) hash.

Ledger

An immutable record of your deposits. Track ownership, timestamps and integrity for every piece of data.

Exchange

License or lend your data under contracts you control. Define duration, scope and anonymization before sharing.

Treasury

Run AI analysis locally to extract insights. Turn your data into value without sacrificing privacy.
